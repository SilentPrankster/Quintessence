DISCOVERING SCIENCE OF SUSTAINABILITY

Paryaवरण - THE MOTHER'S INTERNATIONAL ENVIRONMENT CLUB, is the first of its kind club of our school related purely to activities and drives for enhancing and improving the quality of environment around us. The club is formed out of its two parent club - BIOCOENOSIS and ENVIRONMENT CLUB (SUPW). The clubs main motive is to plant maximum number of trees in school campus in one year and try to enhance and improve the quality of environment around us.
