SYNERGY
IMAGINE - INVENT - INSPIRE

SYNERGY - THE MOTHER'S INTERNATIONAL SCIENCE CLUB, is the brain-child of students and teachers of MIS. A series of events are organised annually by Synergy, ranging from writing science-fiction to building engineering prototypes. These events are held under the supervision of Teachers and the Board of Members. Synergy's main motive is to develop scientific interest and temperament in students in a holistic and engaging manner.
